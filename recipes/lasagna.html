Lasagna

A classic lasagna recipe

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics:

Ingredients:

  • Meat: This super meaty lasagna has sweet italian sausage and ground beef
  • Onion and Garlic: An onion and two cloves of garlic are cooked with the meat to add tons of flavor
  • Tomato products: You'll need a can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce and a tube of tomato paste
  • Sugar: Two tablespoons of sugar adds sutble sweetness to the flavor of the sauce
  • Spices and seasonings: This recipe is flavored with fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, italian seasoning and fennel seeds
  • Lasagna noodles: You can use store-bought or homemade
  • Cheese: Parmesan, mozarella, and ricotta cheese make this lasagna extra decadent
